Is this safe?

In short: use at your own risk.

When you log in with your card number and PIN, we save your information in our database. We encrypt your PIN before saving it. All communications between you and us and between us and the TPL are done over HTTPS. We've taken the most basic of precautions to keep your data safe.

The way that we figure out if your books are due is by "pretending" that we're you. That is, our servers log into your account for you, take a peek at your loans chart, and if they see anything that's coming due, they email you. That's why we have to save your PIN.

If someone does hijack your info, we think they worst they can do is reserve a bunch of books without you knowing it. The TPL charges $1 for every book that you reserve but fail to pick up.

Just to make it clear: We take no responsibility for any theft or loss of library card information as a result of using this site.
